ASPU: from the moment of creation to the present

1923

Armavir State Pedagogical University is one of the oldest educational institutions in the South of Russia, providing professional pedagogical education. Professional pedagogical education began in 1923.

1923

Three large domes with spires and a scaly roof rose above the corner facade of the building, cut through by rows of high arched windows. In general, the appearance of the school was eclectic, combining a classical composition with elements of the "brick style".

late 19th century

In most publications, "brick style" refers to the rationalist trend in historicist architecture of the 1830s–1880s. The substantiation of this term within the framework of rational architecture was given by the teacher of the St. Petersburg School of Civil Engineering A. K. Krasovsky.

1930

The academic building had the following construction and technical parameters: brick walls and foundation; wooden floors; reinforced concrete floors of the cellar and the first floor; wooden plastered ceiling of the second floor; iron roof; total usable area - 2.713 sq. m.; height - 10.4 m.; volume in external outlines – 16.055 cubic meters.

2008

A metal grand staircase leading to the second floor, made at the John-Martin & Co. iron foundry in Rostov-on-Don, was installed in the lobby.

2008

Year 2000. A hall for sports events was built in Armavir State Pedagogical Institute , and in 2008 it was reconstructed.

2012

A fire at the Academy in early 2012 stirred up the whole region. Several hundred square meters were burning. The roof and the assembly hall were completely burnt out. Firefighters "spilled" the building for three days. The library and museum were damaged.

2014

In 2014 Armavir State Pedagogical University was restored to its former appearance, but inside the building has changed significantly, the old staircase reminds of its historical significance.

2021

In 2021, the construction of the TECHNOPARK began at Armavir State Pedagogical University.It was decided to reconstruct the old sports hall located inside the building. Now a technopark is located on the site of the old sports hall.

2022

The technopark was opened in 2022. Today we can observe digital architecture at Armavir State Pedagogical University. The changes concern not only the technological hell, but also a new set of limitless functions: they over estimate the historical heritage of buildings, as well as improve their social purpose. A scenario is rapidly developing where digital interactivity is becoming a desirable feature of new projects or building restoration programs.

2022

The internal architecture of the Armavir Pedagogical University is made in high-tech style.

2022

All these technological solutions indicate an interesting trend: a virtual infrastructure is being created that allows mixing and recognizing the real and the ephemeral, as well as endowing the architecture with powerful functionality and practical benefits.

2022

Architecture is increasingly interpreting the idea of impermanence, and this is the fundamental difference between the cities of the future and the cities of the past. Architecture is increasingly migrating towards managing the ever-changing flow of information. This concept, which represents an expanded vision of the digital age itself, combines the fluidity of design thought with a new ability to create new meanings.
